Both surfaces are brand-led: design IS the product. Site A is a brand-guidelines
hub; Site B is a franchise recruitment landing page. Neither is an app UI.

## Product purpose
A two-site "living" ecosystem that shares one JSON source of truth
(`shared/brand-data.json`). Edit the file once and both sites re-skin, no rebuild.
- **Site A — Creative & Brand Hub:** the brand guidelines (foundations, logo,
  colour, type, voice, imagery, house of brands). The publisher of the SSOT.
- **Site B — Franchise Landing:** recruits multi-unit operators and premium
  landlords. Consumes the same SSOT and re-skins live.

## Users
- **The Host** (primary, end customer): 35–60, building a home they intend to keep
  and fill with people. Sees a game room as where the family actually spends time.
  Wants quality that lasts at a price that reads as value, not luxury tax.
- Designers and builders specifying for clients.
- Multi-unit operators, premium landlords, family offices (Site B audience).

## Brand & tone
- **Voice:** warm, confident, family-rooted, never stuffy. We sell the room a
  family gathers in, not a unit on a warehouse floor.
- **Purpose line:** "Enabling families and friends to share priceless time
  together, one game room at a time." (Family-owned since 1975.)
- **Core values:** Family Focus, Customer Commitment, Community Support,
  Employee Care.
- **Mechanics:** Oxford comma always. Max one exclamation point. Spell one–nine,
  figures for 10+ and all prices. Concrete over clever (name the slate, the
  hardwood, the tolerance).

## Anti-references (what to avoid)
- "Man cave," "game room toys," "cheap," "clearance," "unit."
- Luxury-jargon framing ("luxury lifestyle brand," "curate lifestyles"). The brand
  is warm and family-first, not aspirational-cold. ("No luxury tax" is on-message.)
- AI-slop tells: em dashes, side-stripe accent borders, gradient text, decorative
  glass, the single hero-metric template, identical icon-card grids, flat type
  scales, cold studio/catalog imagery.

## Strategic principles
1. Family-first purpose leads; the vertical-integration / factory-direct story
   supports it (it is the proof, not the headline).
2. One source of truth. Any brand fact lives in `brand-data.json` and is rendered,
   never hardcoded twice.
3. Earn the accent: Signal Orange and urgency are rare on purpose (≤5%).
4. Restraint reads as premium. Depth and texture over flat fills; specifics over
   adjectives.
